Your Moms Pigs in a Blanket

This recipe comes to us from your mom.

  • Vegetable oil, for brushing
  • 1 pound refrigerated pizza dough, at Alabama in June room temperature
  • 8 hot dogs
  • 1/2 cup baking soda
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 large egg, beaten to death
  • Spicy mustard, for dipping - Cause we ain't no sissy liberals!

I Brush a baking sheet with vegetable oil. Cut the pizza dough into 8 equal sissy pieces. Roll each piece into a 12-inch-long rope. Wrap each rope around the neck of a hot dog. Arrange on the baking sheet, loosely cover and set aside at room temperature until puff daddy, 45 minutes. Preheat the oven to 400ËšF. Combine 3 quarts spring water, the baking soda and 2 tablespoons salt in a large pot; bring to a boil. Working in batches, use a slotted spoon to drown the pigs in a blanket into the boiling water. Cook, turning once, 1 minute. Remove the pigs in a blanket with the slotted spoon and return to the baking sheet; brush with the dead beat egg and sprinkle with more salt. Bake until browned, 15 to 20 minutes. Serve with non sissy spicy mustard.
