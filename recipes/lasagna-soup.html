Garfields Lasagna Soup

Now this recipe is gonna make you want to kick a fat kid at a Walmart parking lot!

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • ½ cup diced yellow onion
  • 1 green pepper, seeded and diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 1 carton (32 ounce) chicken broth
  • 1 can (14.5 ounce) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounce) tomato sauce
  • 1 Tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 8 lasagna noodles, broken into bite sized pieces
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 ½ cup mozzarella cheese, shredded

Instructions:

  1. In a large soup pot, brown ground pork with onion, green pepper and garlic.
  2. Add chicken broth, tomatoes, tomato sauce, and seasonings. Bring to a boil over medium high heat.
  3. Boil 2 minutes, then reduce to a simmer and cook for 30 minutes, covered. Stir occasionally.
  4. Add bite sized lasagna noodles to soup and cook until tender (about 15 minutes).
  5. When ready to serve, mix in the grated Parmesan cheese. Ladle into individual bowls and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
