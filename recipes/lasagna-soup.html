Walmart Lasagna Soup

Now this recipe is gonna make you want to kick a fat kid at a Walmart parking lot!

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • ½ cup diced yellow onion
  • 1 green pepper, seeded and diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, pressed
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 large egg, beaten to death
  • Spicy mustard, for dipping - Cause we ain't no sissy liberals!

I Brush a baking sheet with vegetable oil. Cut the pizza dough into 8 equal sissy pieces. Roll each piece into a 12-inch-long rope. Wrap each rope around the neck of a hot dog. Arrange on the baking sheet, loosely cover and set aside at room temperature until puff daddy, 45 minutes. Preheat the oven to 400˚F. Combine 3 quarts spring water, the baking soda and 2 tablespoons salt in a large pot; bring to a boil. Working in batches, use a slotted spoon to drown the pigs in a blanket into the boiling water. Cook, turning once, 1 minute. Remove the pigs in a blanket with the slotted spoon and return to the baking sheet; brush with the dead beat egg and sprinkle with more salt. Bake until browned, 15 to 20 minutes. Serve with non sissy spicy mustard.
